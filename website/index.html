Fyg Cafe Roleplay

Fyg Cafe is a cozy cafe deep in a forest ran by the forest spirit, Fyg! The employees will be happy to serve you, free of charge! Come hang out with our community, with lots of lore and art!

Rules
  1. English only.
  2. Follow Discord TOS and Community Guidelines. This includes harassment, hate speech, threats, evading bans/blocks, sending harmful files, and spam.
  3. Respect your fellow server members.
  4. Follow the roleplay guidelines.
  5. No advertising without prior permission.
  6. Use all channels correctly.
Lexy and the admins have the final say in punishments.
Roleplay Guidelines Roleplay is not required, enjoy the community however you'd like!
  1. Follow your designated role.
  2. Cafe employment applications go to the #applications channel.
  3. Follow the rules of the world. (ex. Don't establish martial law and gonon a murder spree.)
  4. Before you roleplay, write an information page about yourself in the #roleplay-members channel.
  5. Enjoy the cafe and all it has to offer!
