Menu
Drinks
Teas All of our teas are 1st flush and high-quality, supporting local Fillorian farming.
Green Tea
Sencha Tea Sencha is a steamed green tea made from small-leaf Camellia sinensis (tea bushes). Fillorian Sencha tends to have a refreshing flavor that can be described as vegetal, seaweedy, or grassy.
Black Tea
Assam Tea Assam tea is usually described as full bodied and strong with a malty flavor. Full bodied means that tea is rich and complex and is most often related to black tea.
Darjeeling Tea Darjeeling tea is light to medium-bodied with fruity and floral notes, and perhaps a touch of briskness. The steeped liquor usually appears light golden to a darker bronze color with a strong fruity aroma. It is often considered one of the finest teas in the world.
Herbal Tea
Chamomile Tea Chamomile has gentle notes of apple, and there is a mellow, honey-like sweetness in the cup. It has a silky mouthfeel and yet remains a clean, delicately floral herbal tea.
Peppermint Tea Peppermint tea has menthol, which makes the tea taste minty and mildly sweet, leaving a cooling sensation in the mouth. It's light and refreshing.
Oolong Tea
Tin Guan Yin Tea Tie Guan Yin, also known as Iron Goddess of Mercy, is a semi-oxidized oolong tea that has a unique vegetal and refreshing taste. It has depth of flavor and subtle fruitiness.
Dong Ding Tea Dong Ding Oolong is a classic high mountain oolong, it undergoes multiple baking to produce a smooth floral liquor. The complex profile has a floral and toasty edge with notes of exotic flowers, spices, caramelized nuts and wild honey.
Boba All of our boba come with tapioca pearls made with high-quality pure flour. Boba pearls are made of tapioca starch that comes from the cassava root. They're small spheres that are often simmered in a brown sugar syrup to give them a sweet taste and dark black color. They have a chewy texture.
Original Black Milk Tea We use Assam black tea to create our Original Black Milk Tea. It's simple, sweet, and a perfected original.
Brown Sugar Milk Tea This is a boba variant that has no tea in it, just fresh milk, brown sugar syrup and tapioca pearls. The tapioca pearls are cooked on low heat in brown sugar syrup so they absorb all that smoky, caramelly flavour, and these are then topped off with cold fresh milk.
Taro Milk Tea Taro milk tea is a truly delicious variation that has more creamy body thanks to the addition of starchy taro root. Its flavor can be described as sweet and nutty. When taro is cooked, it tastes much similar to sweet potatoes.
Baked Goods
Pastries All of our pastries are lovingly crafted with their respective cultures in mind to pay homage to each of its unique flavor profiles.
Taiyaki Taiyaki is a Japanese fish-shaped cake, commonly sold as street food. It imitates the shape of tai, which it is named after.
Custard Taiyaki This is the basic flavor of taiyaki.
Azuki Taiyaki Azuki can be described as a sweetened red bean paste.
Malasadas Malasadas, as they are known in Hawai'i, are a yeast-leavened doughnut enriched with eggs, butter, and sometimes evaporated or fresh milk. After frying, they are rolled in sugar.
Flores de Hojaldre Flores de hojaldre is a specialty of the Spanish city of Alcalá de Henares, consisting of puff pastry (hojaldre) that has been rolled into beautiful shapes of flowers or roses. At its simplest, flores de hojaldre are distinguished by a light, airy, and flaky texture, and they are usually served dusted with powdered sugar.
Gougère A gougère in French cuisine, is a baked savory choux pastry made of choux dough mixed with cheese. There are many variants. The cheese is commonly grated Gruyère, Comté, or Emmentaler, but there are many variants using other cheeses or other ingredients.
Qottab Qottab is a traditional Iranian almond and walnut-filled crescent pastry that is infused with cardamom and cinnamon flavors to make the perfect treat.
Cherry Croissant A flaky croissant with a sweet cherry filling, drizzled with sweet icing.
Empanadas Empanadas are fried or baked pastries stuffed with sweet or savory fillings. They're known and loved throughout Portugal, the Caribbean, Latin America and the Philippines. The name comes from the Spanish verb empanar, which means to wrap in bread.
Bakery All of our baked goods are made to order with fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors.
Custom Lunchbox Cake Bento cakes, or Lunchbox cakes, are mini cakes not more than four inches in diameter and come nestled in a takeout box. They were first popularised by South Korea and are commonly found in almost all Korean cafes and bakeries. When ordering, include this information:
  • 1-3 custom layers
  • Custom icing
  • Custom fruit filling (optional)
  • Custom design and writing
  • Additional requests (within reason)
Pie
Apple Crumble Pie Our apple crumble pie is sweet but never cloying, with cinnamon. It tastes overwhelmingly of fresh apples, with a bright, clean flavor.
Blueberry Pie Our blueberry pies are baked with warm spices and covered in an intricate lattice flaky crust. We top it with granulated sugar for extra sweetness and texture on top of the pie.
Doughnuts Our perfect donuts are light and fluffy, attributed to its long resting period where the gluten can develop within the dough. Once fried to a golden brown, we top them with sweet glaze or chocolate.
  • Glazed Doughnut
  • Chocolate Eclair Doughnut
Muffins Muffins are a type of sweetbread that are typically eaten for breakfast. We serve cinnamon and blueberry muffins, both sweet and delicious in their respective natures.
  • Cinnamon Muffin
  • Blueberry Muffin
